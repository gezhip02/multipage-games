About Space Shooter

Space Shooter is an exhilarating arcade-style space combat game that puts you in control of a powerful starfighter. Your mission is to defend Earth from waves of alien invaders who are determined to conquer our planet. As the last line of defense, you'll need quick reflexes and strategic thinking to overcome increasingly difficult challenges.

With stunning graphics, explosive effects, and an immersive soundtrack, Space Shooter delivers a truly captivating gaming experience. As you progress through the game, you'll earn upgrades for your ship, unlocking powerful weapons and defensive systems that will help you face the alien armada.

How to Play

  • Controls: Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate your ship. Press Space bar to fire your primary weapon.
  • Power-ups: Collect power-ups dropped by defeated enemies to enhance your firepower, shields, or speed.
  • Objective: Survive as long as possible while defeating enemy ships and bosses to earn points.
  • Pro Tip: Save your special weapons for boss fights and concentrate your fire on the most dangerous enemies first.
